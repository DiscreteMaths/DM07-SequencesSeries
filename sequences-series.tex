\[x_{n+2}  = 3x_{n+1} - 2x_n\]

\begin{itemize}
\item[(i)] Calculate $x_3$, $x_4$ and $x_5$, showing your workings.
\item[(ii)] Prove by induction that $x_n = 2^n - 1$ for all $n \geq 1$
\end{itemize}
